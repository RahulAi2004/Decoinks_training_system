## 2. How we reply — the style that defines a Decoinks agent

**The reply formula — every reply: Acknowledge → Answer → Advance**
1. **Acknowledge** — a warm opener that shows you heard them ("Hey there! 😊", "Great question!").
2. **Answer** — the concrete answer: a real number/fact, or one clarifying question if you need info.
3. **Advance** — push the sale forward: ask for design + size + quantity, offer a mockup, or confirm the next step.

**The 7 core techniques**
1. Always open warm (friendly opener + a light emoji).
2. Answer with a concrete number/fact — not a vague "it depends".
3. Always work toward design + size + quantity (you can't quote without them).
4. Keep it short — 1–3 sentences.
5. Use emojis naturally (😊 🚚 🎉 🔥) — don't spam.
6. Mirror the customer's language — Spanish in → Spanish out.
7. Personalise + reassure — reference what they said; reassure on quality/timing.

## 3. The 5 reply styles (from the Detailed Reply Bank)

Every repeated question has **five** good ways to answer. Pick **one** based on the
customer's mood — never send all five. All five still follow Acknowledge → Answer → Advance.

1. **Direct answer** — give the fact plainly and clearly.
2. **Qualifying reply** — answer, then ask for design / size / quantity to move forward.
3. **Relationship-focused** — build trust and rapport; reassure a hesitant customer.
4. **Value-driven** — frame the offer/benefit (bulk deal, free design, testing a design).
5. **Close-to-order** — push toward the quote/mockup/payment/production next step.

**Worked example — "What is the minimum order quantity?"**
- *Direct:* "Our minimum starts at $25, roughly 60 inches of DTF transfers. Send your artwork and the size and I'll help you fit it smartly."
- *Qualifying:* "Yes, you can start small! Are you after DTF transfers, a gang sheet, or finished shirts? Send the design, size, and quantity and I'll guide you."
- *Relationship:* "No worries — you don't need a big order to start. Many customers begin with the minimum, approve the quality, then scale up."
- *Value-driven:* "The $25 minimum lets you test your design without a big commitment. Share the design and I'll help you calculate how many prints fit."
- *Close-to-order:* "Send me the artwork, the size for each print, and how many pieces — I'll check the layout and get you an exact quote."

## 5. How replies are graded (the standard)

Each intern reply is scored 0–100 on six dimensions, judged against this Source of Truth:

1. **Accuracy** — every fact matches this document. A wrong or invented fact = 0–20 and a violation.
2. **Completeness** — gathered what's needed (design + size + quantity; address for shipping) and answered the question.
3. **Tone** — warm, human, concise (1–3 sentences), light emoji; not robotic.
4. **Policy** — no invented prices/dates; no free physical samples (mockups only); payment details only after approval; honest about what we don't offer.
5. **Language** — replied in the customer's language.
6. **Sales** — moved the sale forward with a clear next step or closing question (Advance).

The ideal reply always follows **Acknowledge → Answer → Advance**, states only facts from
this document, and reads like one of the 5 reply styles above.
